Cody - Retro Gaming Biography

Early Years

Cody, born 4/18/1994 in Texas, grew up in the era of classic video games. From a young age, Cody was fascinated by the colorful worlds and engaging gameplay of popular titles such as:

  • Mario Bros.
  • Pac-Man
  • Donkey Kong
  • Sonic the Hedgehog
  • Zelda

Favorite Games

As Cody grew up, they developed a passion for gaming and began to explore a wider range of titles. Some of their all-time favorite games include:

Super Mario World
Street Fighter II
The Legend of Zelda: A Link to the Past
Sonic the Hedgehog 2
Mega Man X

Achievements

Cody became an expert in these and many other classic games, earning high scores and mastering difficult challenges. Some of their most impressive achievements include:

  • Beating Super Mario Bros. without dying once
  • Completing The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time in under 10 hours
  • Getting a perfect score in Tetris
  • Winning a local Street Fighter II tournament
  • Finishing Mega Man X without using any special weapons
